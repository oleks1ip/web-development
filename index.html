Our History

Our journey began in my cozy home kitchen, where the joy of baking first took root. Inspired by the aroma of freshly baked cookies and the happiness of loved ones sharing desserts, I turned my passion into a craft. Starting with family recipes passed down through generations, I slowly introduced my own twists and flavors. Over the years, this passion blossomed into a home confectionery dedicated to creating sweet memories for every occasion. Whether it's a birthday, a celebration, or just a treat-yourself day, every product carries a piece of my love for the art of baking.
